Return & Replacement Policy

We certainly accept return and also replace of products/items except that they should fall under the policy terms and conditions.

Returns :
Any product can be return or the entire order can be returned if received in a physically damaged condition. A valid proof of video footage has to be submitted to the website user account and/or to the customer support team before unpacking the order.

Any product can be returned if found damaged internally by means of worms, fungus, dust, dirt, odor. A valid proof to be submitted in the website user account and/or with the customer support showcasing the product damage.

Once product reaches us we send the same to lab for testing to verify if the product is sold from us. This is just to avoid any kind of illegal filling of other material in our company pouches. If found the material is not our product, legal proceedings will follow.

Return policy is applicable if the products are placed in the environment and packing that supports them. Individual products has instructions on packing and storage conditions.

Refund for the returned products will be honored to the same account from which the transaction is made or customer support may request to give details of any other way of transfer.

Refunds will be honored in 2-3 business working days.

Replacement policy is applicable if the products are placed in the environment and packing that supports them. Individual products has instructions on packing and storage conditions.
